Signal 1, FAQ gaps

Questions guests ask that the website doesn't answer

How long is the tour?
Came up in 5 of 20 sampled reviews. The Tours page does not state duration. Travelers slot this into a North Shore loop and need a number before they book.
What if it rains, or what if I need to reschedule?
Tours page has no weather, cancellation, or refund line. Footer links to Refund Policy but it is not in front of the booking button. Lokoea Farms publishes both through Viator.
Is the wagon stroller-friendly, can grandma climb on, what about kids under 5?
Came up in 3 of 20 sampled reviews and in the 2026 family blog. Site says nothing.
Will the tour run if there are only two of us?
Not addressed. Lokoea Farms' Viator listing publishes a minimum-traveler refund clause. Kahuku Farms does not.
If I drove from Honolulu, will the mochi still be there at 2pm?
Reviewers say no. The site does not say so, the operator could turn this complaint into urgency by surfacing it.
Signal 2, Comp parity

Where you stack up against Lokoea Farms and Dole Plantation

Themes you own

4-generation Matsuda-Fukuyama family story.Shows up in 14 of 20 of your reviews. Dole Plantation reviewers say "tourist trap" and "giant gift shop." Lokoea has 0 TripAdvisor reviews on its main listing.
Tractor-wagon tasting format.12 of 20 of your reviews. Lokoea is a walking tour, no wagon. Dole has the Pineapple Express train, reviewed as "45 minute lines, not worth it."
Same-property cafe with named menu items.10 of 20 of your reviews call out specific items. Lokoea has no cafe. Dole has commodity food.
"Better than Dole" is in your reviews, unprompted.JulieMeagan and others use Dole as the contrast point. Free positioning.

Themes they own that you don't

Tour duration stated up front.Lokoea via Viator: "2h." Kahuku Farms tour page: blank.
Published cancellation and weather policy.Lokoea via Viator publishes a full-refund clause for weather and minimum-traveler shortfalls. Kahuku Farms does not surface this on the tour page.
Multi-platform OTA distribution.Lokoea is on Viator, Airbnb Experiences, GetYourGuide. Kahuku Farms is FareHarbor-direct only with stale OTA listings. Comp wins on cruise-pax discoverability.
Volume default for the trip slot.Dole Plantation has 6,671 TripAdvisor reviews at 3.6 of 5. Kahuku Farms has 505 at 4.8. You win on quality, you lose on visibility, and the booking page does not surface the contrast.
Signal 3, CRO fixes

Three fixes, ranked

1
Add a one-liner above the FareHarbor button: "About 90 minutes. Tractor wagon ride, two tasting stops, ends at the cafe."
Tours page (kahukufarms.com/pages/tours), directly above the FareHarbor embed.
Impact: HighEffort: Low
"The 1.75 hour farm tour with tastings of fruits grown there and chocolates made from their cacao trees was exceptional."A B, TripAdvisor, Feb 2026. Plus comp gap, Lokoea publishes "2h" on Viator, Kahuku Farms publishes nothing.
2
Add a weather and refund line beside the Book button: "Tours run rain or shine. Free reschedule up to 48 hours before. Full refund if we cancel for weather or if minimums aren't met."
Tours page, in a small block immediately right of the FareHarbor button on desktop, directly below it on mobile.
Impact: HighEffort: Low
Comp gap. Lokoea Farms via Viator publishes: "weather-related cancellations offer a different date or full refund, and if minimum traveler requirements aren't met, customers receive a different date / experience or full refund." Kahuku Farms hides this in a footer policy link.Source, Lokoea Viator product page, 2026.
3
Turn the cafe sell-out into a "Plan your visit" strip with a Square pre-order CTA: "Mochi and banana bread sell out by 1pm. Pre-order online to hold yours."
Homepage cafe section and Tours page sidebar. Reuse existing Square online order flow, no new tech.
Impact: MediumEffort: Low
"Do remember to get there early to be able to experience the mochi before it sells out."Alex D., Hayward CA, on-site testimonial. Reviewers already warn each other. Owning it converts a quiet complaint into urgency and lifts pre-orders.
