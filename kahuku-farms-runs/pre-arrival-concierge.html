Anxiety map · 5 rows operations pillar
Closed-toe shoes, dirt-path footing
Refund pressure if guests show up in flip-flops on a working farm. Surface early so they pack right.
T-7
Tour duration unclear
Cancellation risk and low-NPS. Travelers can't slot the farm into a North Shore-loop day plan when "how long" is missing on the site.
T-7
Sun, water, bathroom on a working farm
Refund pressure on a hot day. One prep line at T-7 (hat, water, the bathroom is at the café) prevents 80% of inbound calls.
T-7
Mobility on dirt and wagon steps
Low-NPS and refund risk for older guests, strollers, or anyone with a knee. T-1 confirms what the wagon and field are actually like.

Weather and rescheduling on an open-air wagon
No-show and refund pressure on borderline-weather mornings. Day-of weather call with one-tap reschedule cuts both.

3 service-flow upgrades to ship into the booking flow
01
Add-on field at FareHarbor checkout
FareHarbor (item 9887) supports add-on line items at checkout. Wire two: "Hold banana bread + mochi for our group" and "Add a farm gift box, shipped." Captures upsell at the moment of highest commitment, no email needed for the first conversion.
EffortLow ImpactHigh
02
Pre-arrival "what to wear, what to expect, where to park" page
One URL on kahukufarms.com, linked from every touch and from the FareHarbor confirmation. Covers shoes, sun, water, bathroom location, parking, tour duration, mobility (wagon steps, dirt paths), and the weather call protocol. Eliminates the inbound calls and the policy gap on the current tour page.
EffortLow ImpactMid
03
T+0 morning weather-call SMS with parking pin and reschedule link
Two-line SMS sent 2 to 3 hours before the wagon rolls. Confirms today's call (rolling or rescheduling), drops a parking pin, and gives a one-tap reschedule link if the morning's a wash. Replaces the 90% of "I can't find you" and "is it still on?" calls, removes the refund pressure on borderline-weather days.
