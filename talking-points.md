## SLIDE 3 — The Guest Journey Framework (~2 min)

**On screen:** 5-stage journey bar with questions

**This is the framework they'll use mentally from now on. Teach it.**

> "Every hotel has money hiding somewhere in a guest's journey. We break it into five stages."

Walk through each briefly:

> "Discover — how do guests even find you? Book — when they're ready, do they pick you or an OTA? Arrival — are you selling before check-in? Stay — do guests leave as advocates? Reconnect — do past guests come back?"

> "A 20-room motel and a 200-room resort have the same five stages. The gaps just show up in different places."

**CALIBRATED QUESTION:**

> "When a member tells you 'bookings are down' — which of these five stages do you think they're usually stuck in?"

**(PAUSE — 7 seconds. Let someone answer. Acknowledge it.)**

> "Usually Discover or Book. That's where most of the money hides."

**Transition:**

> "This is the method. Now let me show you what it looks like in practice."

**[ADVANCE]**

## Q&A Prep — Likely Questions

**"How long does the workshop take?"**
Two sessions, typically 60-90 minutes each, spread over 1-2 weeks.

**"What size hotel is this for?"**
20 rooms to 200 rooms. The framework scales. We work with micro properties and larger independents.

**"Do you work with chains/brands?"**
Independent and boutique hotels. That's our lane.

**"How do I refer a member?"**
Intro email to kin@lightson.co, or share the website checkup tool and we'll follow up.

**"What if they already have a revenue manager / marketing agency?"**
We can complement or replace. The diagnostic will show where the gaps are regardless.

**"What markets do you cover?"**
Currently HI, CA, MT, WY, VT, FL. Washington would be new — that's why we joined WHA.
